Auto Moderator

The TOS Auto Moderator is a Google Chrome extension designed to automate the moderation process in the Trial System of an online game. Leveraging machine learning algorithms and built-in logic, the extension casts votes on player reports with a high degree of accuracy. Created to streamline the report judgment process, this extension assumes that most users submit accurate report details, based on which it carries out its voting mechanism.

Features:
  • Voting Modes: Everything innocent, 80% innocent, smart voting, skip everything except lists of specific reports
  • Skip Modes: Linear, random, skip every report until certain report ID
  • Different behaviors according to report category

Reading Club

  • Planning and executing theater plays: Managing plays (including characters), manage planned plays; manage scoring of actors and plays
